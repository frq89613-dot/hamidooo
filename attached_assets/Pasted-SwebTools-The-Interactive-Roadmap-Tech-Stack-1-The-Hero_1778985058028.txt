4. Technical Specification (The Modern Stack)
Floating Tech Icons: "Display the tech stack (Next.js, Supabase, Groq) as floating 3D badges that subtly bob up and down."

Speed Meter: "Next to the Performance section, add a Live Speed Gauge that animates from 0 to 99 to visually represent our 95+ PageSpeed target."

5. Advanced Directives for the Developer (The "Action" Instructions):
Framer Motion Integration: "Use Framer Motion for all transitions. Every button click and page scroll must have a smooth, spring-based motion. No sharp or boring transitions."

Glassmorphism: "Apply a Glassmorphism effect (blurred transparent backgrounds) to all dashboards and pop-ups to make the UI feel deep and multi-layered."

Scroll-Triggered Animations: "Implement ScrollReveal. Elements should only come to life (fade in, scale up) exactly when they enter the user's viewport."

Dark/Light Toggle: "Add a custom-animated toggle switch for Dark Mode that changes the entire site's energy from 'Deep Space Blue' to 'Clean Lab White' with a smooth sweep effect."
